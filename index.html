FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What types of AI models do you use?

We utilize a variety of state-of-the-art AI models including GPT-4 for text generation, DALL-E for image creation, and specialized models for code generation and data analysis. Our models are continuously updated to ensure you get the best results.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We employ enterprise-grade encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Your generated content is never shared with third parties or used to train our models without your explicit consent. For enterprise customers, we offer additional security options including private deployments.

How does the free trial work?

Our free trial gives you full access to all tools for 14 days with a limited number of requests per day. No credit card is required to start the trial. At the end of the trial period, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan or continue with our free tier which has reduced capabilities.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel, you'll continue to have access to the paid features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will automatically switch to the free tier.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit or educational status to learn about available discounts.
